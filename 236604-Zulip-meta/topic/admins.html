Mario Carneiro (May 09 2020 at 04:22):

The main reason I've kept it small is that there aren't actually that many things that a Zulip admin is required to do, demand is low (by contrast to mathlib maintainers, which are dealing with the flood of multiple PRs per day).

Mario Carneiro (May 09 2020 at 04:23):

Also I don't think there has ever been an issue requiring immediate attention from an admin, so it's not a huge problem to wait for one to wake up.
